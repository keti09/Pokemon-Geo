The First Pokémon Region...

The Kanto region from the Red and Blue series of the game is actually based on the real life Kanto region of Japan, made from the Greater Tokyo Area, with the map in the Pokémon games being nearly geographically similar, with the western portion of the map actually derived from the eastern Chūbu area of Japan.

Pallet Town is based off Pokémon series creator Satoshi Tajiri hometown, Pewter City inspired by the famous Iwajuku site and Maebashi. Saffron City is based on the Marunouchi Area of Tokyo, with the Magnet train station in the games being based off the Tokyo Station bullet train that travels between Tokyo and Osaka. in the image you can see how the game version overlays to the Greater Tokyo Area. In Red and Blue Version you are essentially exploring the most populous region of Japan.
